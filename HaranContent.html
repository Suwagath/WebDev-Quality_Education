Global Impact and Success Stories

Impact On Economy
Education is defined as the stock of skills, competencies and other productivity-enhancing characteristics.Quality education enhances economic growth by improving their individual skill and knowledge which is essential for job opportunities.Moreover,the more the individual knowledge the more his contribution will benefit the growth of economic society.With no doubt quality education is the foundation of the advance society which we are yet to for see.
Impact On Health System
Health education is an extremely broad field and it can be presented under numerous different circumstances.Health education is one of the respected education which is challenging at the same time rewarding as well.For this reason,with no question quality education must be implemented in the system.The higher quality education an individual gets the high skill he gain,meaning he can save a lot of people in our society.For example, the only people who were given prioraty in corana pandemic were the doctor's,the respect for a doctor would last forever.The higher the quality health education the more we advance to a strong society which can save many lives.
Impact On Poverty
If all children in low-income countries left school able to read, global poverty would fall by 12 %.In addition to this,student's would hone their knowledge and skills where they could solve the crisis of there low poverty in their area by finding new business deals,creating their own business and so on.This is why the UN has taken an approach in giving quality education to low-poverty students.
Story of Finland
In the 1970s, Finland undertook a series of education reforms aimed at improving the quality of education for all students.The main goal was give equal opportunities for rich and poor.As well as, rules were implemented that the requirement to teachers to teach was a masters degree for higher quality education.This resulted in Finnish students consistently rank among the top performers in international assessments such as the Programme for International Student Assessment (PISA). They excel in reading, mathematics, and science.Furthermore, the fact there were students who had a low profile background but still made it PISA is a huge amusment.UN encouraged this and made arragement to improvice it worldwide were this website is also the step stone to make that come to reality.
